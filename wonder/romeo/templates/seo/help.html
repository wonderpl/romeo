I Can Haz Sitemapz?

Robots.txt file setup

Search engines crawl your content via the robot.txt in the root of your website.

Below are steps to get your WonderPl hosted video content accessible to the world through SERPs.

Embed some video

On your website, embed a video by pasting the embed code here in to the page on your blog or website.

Add a url to click-to-more

On the video details screen (here) in the click-to-more field, add in the url of the page that the video will be embedded in.

Update your robots.txt file

Add the line: Sitemap: http://wonderpl.com/seo/sitemaps/<account-id>.xml to the end of your robots.txt file (replacing <account-id> with your account id - OBVS!) and any new pages which are referenced in your videos will be crawled.
